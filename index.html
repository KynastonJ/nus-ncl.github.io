National Cybersecurity Lab (NCL)

The National Cybersecurity Lab (NCL) is an experimentation environment that is augmented with valuable data and applications. It is currently funded by the National Research Foundation (NRF) of Singapore.

The NCL aims to provide a platform that fosters and encourages collaboration among researchers involved in academia, the industry as well as government bodies both locally and internationally through the sharing of research data.

We believe that the experimentation environment will help push technological frontiers not only in terms of cybersecurity but also help in teaching and the passing on of knowledge.

The NCL currently has 10 nodes, and we are constantly growing and adding more nodes. Through our continuous R&D efforts, we are constantly improving and enhancing the infrastructure at various layers which include the underlying experimental environment provisioning system to the applications and data repository.

At all stages of development, the NCL strives to have effective and transparent governance of resources in terms of infrastructure and data collection.
