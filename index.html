National Cybersecurity Lab (NCL)

The NCL is an experimentation environment that is augmented with valuable data and applications. In addition, the NCL is a platform for collaboration among researchers from the academia, industry, and government agencies in Singapore. The main objectives of the NCL operations are as follows.

  1. Cost-effective setup and maintenance of the infrastructure
  2. Effective and transparent governance of resources, including the infrastructure and data collection
  3. Enhancement of the infrastructure at various layers through continuous R&D efforts; layers from the underlying experimental environment provisioning system to the applications and data repository
  4. Building up a user community to facilitate the sharing of research data and fostering collaborations among researchers, both locally and internationally
